Caleb Bislow

Caleb began following Christ at an early age in the sticks of Nebraska. While others were partying on the weekends he spent most of his time at church or on his dirt bike. After fully committing his life to Christ, his love for adventure and risk on the motocross track soon transferred to his faith. Since then, he has been humbled to advance the Kingdom of Christ on every inhabited continent in the world.
Caleb is passionate about empowering an army of Unusual Soldiers to take the love of Christ into dark, dangerous, and despised places. He strives to practice what he has the opportunity to preach at a wide variety of venues. Through creativity, humor, and adventurous stories Caleb connects with audiences of various ages challenging them to live out a life of total surrender to Christ.

Caleb is an ordained minister and a graduate of Bethel College in Mishawaka, Indiana. He served as a Youth Pastor for eight years prior to joining Kingdom Building Ministries as a full-time missionary and itinerant speaker. He lives in Franklin, Nebraska with his wife, Jessica, sons Trace and Asher, and daughter Kaiya. To learn more about Caleb visit www.unusualsoldiers.com.

Audience Captivity & Event Availability

Caleb's ministry generally focuses on Senior High, College, and Adult age groups where he can evangelize, encourage, equip, and empower them to live all out for Christ. He is available for the following types of events.
  • Camps
  • Retreats
  • Missions Conferences
  • Youth Conferences
  • Crusades
  • Training Conferences
  • Adventure & Wilderness Ministries
  • Church Services
  • Jail & Prison Ministries
  • Music Festivals
  • Mission Trips
  • Men’s Groups
  • High School & College Spiritual Emphasis Weeks

Ministry Passions

  • To equip an army of Christ followers to serve God wholeheartedly and courageously
  • To globally reach for the unreached and the “unreachables” in the hidden parts of the world
  • To hold speaking engagements that commission movements
  • To empower people to live a lifestyle that listens to God’s voice and follows Him no matter what the cost
  • To arm believers with love and take them to places that are feared, neglected, or avoided by Christians

Top Messages

  • AWAKE: Stepping out of a dry existence; stepping into being alive
  • TRUE IDENTITY: Unleashing the warrior spirit that lies inside of us
  • PASSION: Loving Christ more than the air we breathe
  • VISION: Joining God in the pursuit of His world-wide dream
  • COURAGE: Risking it all for the sake of God’s call on your life
  • THE VOICE: How to Listen to God’s Voice
  • THE FAITH VENTURE: Following God’s voice no matter what the cost
  • EMPOWERED: Living in the Fullness of the Holy Spirit
  • THE 13th FLOOR: Taking the love of Christ to feared places
  • THE POINT OF NO RETURN: Totally surrendering all that you are to God
