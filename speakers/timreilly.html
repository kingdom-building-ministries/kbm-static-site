Tim Reilly

Tim was an antagonistic atheist growing up after his mom died of cancer when he was 8 years old. After debating and arguing many Christians out of their faith, God got a hold of Tim through his intellect first; based on the evidence of the Resurrection. Then he had a radical experience on 6/13/01, in a church of all places. Tim was very successful in the insurance industry for over a decade, but throughout that time God had been growing and preparing Tim to be a full time minister of the Gospel.
On 11/1/12 he stepped out of his insurance profession and began full time ministry leading an evangelistic equipping ministry called Compelled. Compelled exists to equip followers of Christ to share their faith in an intentional, relational and relatable way. Tim is passionate about letting people of all ages know that they do not have to check their minds at the door to trust and follow Jesus.

Tim is married to his best friend Erin, and they have 3 beautiful daughters (Reagan who is 6, Lorelai who is 4, and Evangeline who is almost 2) and a 4th child due in November. Tim is a pastor/evangelist/missionary at a church in Morgan Hill, California (Bay Area) called Morgan Hill Bible Church. Tim loves cycling, apologetics, theology and people. To learn more about Tim, visit iamcompelled.org and timreilly316.com
