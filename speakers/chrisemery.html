Chris Emery

Chris has ministered to students, young adults, and adults from small church to mega church venues. He has been a Student Ministry Pastor, Church Planter, and Lead Pastor. He grew up in San Diego and has ministered from New York to North Carolina. Chris is a graduate of Indiana Wesleyan University.

Along with speaking around the country, Chris serves as Executive Pastor at The Tabernacle in Buckley, MI. Chris also sits on the Board of Directors for White Stone Project and Big Ticket Festival.

Chris lives in Buckley, MI with his wife Doree and four children; Brock, Garrett, Taylor, and Jordyn. For more information, visit www.chris-emery.com
