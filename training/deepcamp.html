deep adj. \ˈdēp\

  1. very intense or extreme
  2. profound or penetrating in awareness or understanding
Deep Camp is a multi-generational 5-day discipleship training experience designed to take people deep, past the familiar and into a world of EXTREME devotion to God, while equipping them to live with hearts on fire and lives on purpose in their everyday realities.

What is Deep Camp?

Throughout the week the participants will…
  • Encounter a VISION of what it means to live a life in pursuit of God
  • Be CHALLENGED to surrender to His calling
  • Be EQUIPPED with tools and next steps to follow God’s call on their life, and...
  • Be MOBILIZED to engage their homes, schools, churches and communities as a Kingdom laborer.
The Speakers, Worship Band, and Staff at Deep Camp will assist participants in stripping away the layers of falsehood and distractions that are evident in everyday life. During camp sessions, small group discussions, Rocky Mountain activities, and one-on-one interactions, the Deep staff will encourage, walk alongside, and pray for participants as they answer God’s call to deeper devotion.

Who is Deep Camp for?

Deep Camp is open to all ages and families, offering specific discipleship "tracks" for children, youth, young adults and adults.
  • Families (both married and married with children) are welcome. There will be discipleship tracks specifically designed for adults, youth, and elementary and toddler aged children. Childcare will be provided for infants.
  • Individuals (High school students - who have at least completed freshman year of high school, young adults, and adults) who have given their lives to Christ and desire to grow spiritually are welcome to attend.
  • Youth Groups are welcomed at Deep Camp! Students completing their 8th grade year and younger must be in attendance with a chaperone.
  • Both stateside and international students and families (fluent in English) are encouraged to attend Deep Camp. As a result, this international, multi-ethnic camp is prepared to receive students flying from a distance to attend.

Where is Deep Camp hosted?

Deep Camp will be hosted at YMCA's premier lodge facility, Snow Mountain Ranch in the rugged Colorado Rocky Mountains less than two hours from Denver's International Airport! Both lodge and camping accommodations are available. View Pricing & Discount details for more information.

Lodge accommodations are available! (Childcare will be provided.)
